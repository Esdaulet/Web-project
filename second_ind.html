    About Dombra

    The dombra is a traditional musical instrument that holds great cultural and historical significance, particularly in Kazakhstan and Central Asia. It is a two-stringed, long-necked lute-like instrument with a pear-shaped body, and it is often regarded as a symbol of Kazakh culture.
